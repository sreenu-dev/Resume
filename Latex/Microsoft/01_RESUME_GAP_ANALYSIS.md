## Resume Transformation for Microsoft

### Rule 1: Lead With Growth Mindset
Microsoft interviews for people who **learn, adapt, and improve continuously**. Every story should show a moment of growth, learning from failure, or adapting to change.

**Before:**
> "Deployed multi-agent AI workflows to automate boilerplate scaffolding."

**After (Microsoft framing):**
> "Self-taught LangGraph and multi-agent orchestration frameworks, applying new knowledge to deploy AI-driven scaffolding workflows that cut feature time-to-market by 25% — demonstrating that the right technical investment compounds over time."

Microsoft deeply values engineers who **break silos** and work across team boundaries.

**After:**
