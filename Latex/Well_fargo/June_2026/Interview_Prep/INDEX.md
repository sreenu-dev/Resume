## 🎯 Quick Reference

### Interview Stages
1. **Phone Screen** (30 min) - Recruiter screening
2. **Technical Screen** (45-60 min) - Coding or system design
3. **Technical Interview** (60 min) - System design or architecture
4. **Behavioral Interview** (45-60 min) - Leadership and teamwork
5. **Final Round** (60-90 min) - Manager/director interview

### Success Factors
- **Technical Skills:** 40%
- **Communication:** 30%
- **Behavioral:** 20%
- **Culture Fit:** 10%

### Key Preparation Areas
1. Coding problems (30-50 problems)
2. System design (5-15 problems)
3. Behavioral stories (5-7 stories)
4. Wells Fargo knowledge
5. Mock interviews (3-10 sessions)
