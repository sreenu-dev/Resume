## 🎓 Interview Types at Wells Fargo

### 1. **Phone Screen (30 minutes)**

- Background and experience questions

### 2. **Technical Phone Screen (45-60 minutes)**
- Coding problem or system design
- Live coding on CoderPad or similar
- Explanation of your approach
- Code optimization discussion

### 3. **Technical Interview (60 minutes)**
- System design OR coding problem
- Whiteboarding or live coding

- Problem-solving examples
- Conflict resolution

### 5. **Final Round (60-90 minutes)**
- Senior engineer or manager interview
- Deep technical discussion
- Culture fit assessment
- Compensation discussion

## 🔑 Key Interview Tips

### Do's ✅
- **Clarify requirements** before starting
- **Think out loud** so interviewers understand your approach
- **Ask questions** about the problem or system
- **Discuss trade-offs** and explain your choices
- **Write clean code** with good variable names
- **Test your solution** with examples
- **Be honest** if you don't know something
- **Show enthusiasm** for the role and company
- **Listen carefully** to feedback and hints
- **Follow up** after the interview

### Don'ts ❌
- **Don't rush** into coding without understanding the problem
- **Don't write messy code** that's hard to read
- **Don't ignore edge cases** and error handling
- **Don't be defensive** about feedback
- **Don't memorize solutions** - understand the concepts
- **Don't talk too much** without letting interviewer speak
- **Don't be negative** about previous employers
- **Don't ask about salary** in technical interviews
- **Don't check your phone** during the interview
- **Don't lie** about your experience or skills

## 💡 Common Interview Questions

### Technical Questions
1. "Design a payment processing system"
2. "Design a distributed ledger"
3. "How would you handle high-throughput transactions?"
4. "Design a secure API gateway"
5. "How would you optimize database queries?"

### Behavioral Questions
1. "Tell me about a time you improved system performance"
2. "Describe a situation where you had to work with a difficult team member"
3. "How do you approach learning new technologies?"
4. "Tell me about a time you failed and what you learned"
5. "How do you ensure code quality and security?"

### Wells Fargo Specific
1. "Why do you want to work at Wells Fargo?"
2. "How would you handle working in a regulated environment?"
3. "Tell me about your experience with compliance and security"
4. "How do you approach building trustworthy systems?"
